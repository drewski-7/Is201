About Me

What makes me, well me.

I've identified three pivotal attributes that I value in a prospective career: a focus on relationships, a commitment to service, and an emphasis on problem-solving. Wealth management, as a potential career path, encapsulates all three of these criteria for me.

My conviction in this belief is grounded in the valuable insights I've gained through my experiences. During my two-year tenure as a humanitarian missionary in Colombia, I grasped the profound impact achievable through individual relationships. As a licensed insurance agent specializing in life insurance, I witnessed firsthand the significant assistance financial services can provide to individuals within our community. Furthermore, my role in leading a team at Vivint equipped me with a deep understanding of the challenges, joys, and rewards inherent in effective problem-solving.

My experience with each of these fields and my desire to learn and gain experience will make me a perfect candidate to boost any private wealth management firm in its desire to serve its clientele.
